Correlation Between Poverty and Obesity

Denver- Recently released data from the CDC today indicates that there is a direct correlation between the poverty rate and the rate of obesity. Information gathered from every state shows that there is a direct relationship between the two.

In reviewing the information, the states with both the highest rates of obesity and poverty gravitate toward the traditional South, however, states like New Mexico and Ohio have started to make their way up.

States like Colorado, Hawai'i, and Massachusetts are showing the lowest rates of the nation.
